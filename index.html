Why the Black Swan

Rare events reshape everything

In probability theory, a Black Swan is an event that is rare, carries extreme and often unpredictable impact, and is only rationalized in hindsight.

Moments like the public emergence of large language models show how quickly foundational assumptions can shift, accelerating change across science and medicine in ways few fully anticipated.

It only takes a single observation, a single event, to shatter certainty.

In science and medicine, the most consequential insights often follow this same pattern. They are hidden in messy, real-world data, invisible to conventional methods, and transformative once revealed.

Our Mission

the signals that change practice

We apply AI and causal inference to surface evidence hidden in real-world data, so better decisions reach patients sooner. Our work spans strategy, tooling, and training across the full evidence lifecycle.

What We Offer

AI & RWE Strategy Consulting

Advisory services at the intersection of artificial intelligence, real-world evidence, and causal inference to help organizations design rigorous, regulatory-ready evidence programs.

Bespoke AI Tool Development

Purpose-built AI tools that streamline real-world data curation, analysis pipelines, and evidence generation workflows, tailored to your therapeutic area and data environment.
